A gallery of examples showcasing complications that arise in mass digitisations of books

Artefacts

Pages where OCR engines detect "texts" that do not actually exist

False paragraphs

Examples where OCR engines arbitrarily introduce paragraph breaks in text

Margin texts

Texts in page margins like header, page number, footer, footnotes, etc.

Complex layouts

Complex layouts such as tables, double or triple column layouts, etc.


Lists

Structured lists with and without numbers/bullet points

Quotes

Quoted texts either in blocks or inline, verses, etc.

Images

Text recognised by the OCR engine from regions of page containing non-textual content like images, plots, etc.

Multilingual content

Text in more than one language, either in separate paragraphs or interspersed within the text.
